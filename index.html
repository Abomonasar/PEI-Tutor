Commitment & Payment:
To ensure consistent progress and academic growth, students are enrolled on a monthly basis for the entire academic year. Fees are billed at the beginning of each month, providing a stable and supportive learning environment.

Discontinuation:
Should you wish to discontinue tutoring, please provide notice at least one week before the next billing cycle. This policy helps us maintain a consistent schedule and ensures that each student’s spot is reserved..

Missed Sessions:
Sessions missed by the student are not refundable, but with at least 24 hours’ notice, a makeup session may be offered if availability allows.
