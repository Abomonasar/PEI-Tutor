About Me

Hello! I'm Amin, a dedicated math tutor offering structured, full-course math tutoring for high school students. I hold a BSc, MSc, and PhD in Engineering, with extensive experience teaching math courses to school, college, and university students. I’ve been recognized by various institutions for my best teaching practices, which emphasize clarity, engagement, and student success. Whether online or in-person in Charlottetown, PEI, I help students build confidence and excel academically.

Teaching Strategy: I use a personalized, one-on-one approach to identify each student's strengths and areas for improvement. Math skills are developed gradually, building a strong foundation and progressing in alignment with the school curriculum — or ahead of it, when possible. My goal is to enhance understanding and performance through consistent, tailored support.
